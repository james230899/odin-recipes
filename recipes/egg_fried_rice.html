Egg Fried Rice

Description

This easy egg fried rice is a perfect go-to meal for busy days. Made with leftover rice, scrambled eggs, and soy sauce, itâ€™s a delicious, no-fuss dish that comes together in just 10 minutes.

Ingredient

  • White Rice
    • >
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Eggs
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a skillet. Scramble eggs and set aside
  2. Stir-fry the rice: Heat remaining oil. Add rice and fry for 2-3 minutes, breaking up clumps
  3. Combine and season: Add eggs back in, pour in soy sauce, and mix well. Season to taste
  4. Finish: Toss in green onions if using. Serve hot.

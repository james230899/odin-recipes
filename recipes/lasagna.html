Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known if its delicious sauce and sheets of pasta

Ingredients

Meat Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Beef Mince
  • Tomato
  • Tomato Paste
  • Red Wine
  • Seasonings
    • Beef stock
    • Bay leaves
    • Thyme
    • Oregano
White Sauce
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
Assembly
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

Meat Sauce

  1. In a pot over medium heat cook Garlic, Onion, Celery, and Carrot for ten minutes
  2. Add beef mince, break up as it cooks
  3. Once browned, add remaining Meat Sauce ingredients.
  4. Once bubbling gently, put the lid on and cook for 2 hours. Stir occassionally
  5. Remove lid and simmer for 30 minutes

Cheese Sauce

  1. Warm up milk
  2. Melt butter in large saucepan over medium-low heat. Add flour and mix constantly for 1 minute
  3. Pour 1 cup of milk, mix in with flour. Once lump free add remaining milk and wisk
  4. Turn heat up to medium-high. Stir occasisionally, more frequently as sauce thickens, 5-8 minutes
  5. Remove from heat, add remaining cheese sauce ingredients

Assemble

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F
  2. Using a large baking dish, add some meat sauce to the bottom then cover with lasagna sheets.
  3. Add just enough meat sauce ot cover these sheets, add 1 cup of cheese sauce.
  4. Top with lasagna sheets, repeat this process 2 more times.
  5. Top with last layer of lasagna sheets, pour over remaining cheese sauce.
  6. Sprinkle with cheese of choice, bake for 30 minutes until golden.
  7. Stand for 5 to 10, then Enjoy!
